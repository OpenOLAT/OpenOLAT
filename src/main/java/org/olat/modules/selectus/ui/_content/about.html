The Faculty Recruiting Tool software has been developed jointly by ETH Zurich and frentix GmbH based on business processes and workflows implemented at ETH Zurichs Office for Faculty Affairs. Requirements analysis, concept, design and implementation of the Faculty Recruiting Tool was done by frentix GmbH. The Faculty Recruiting Tool is used in production since 2010 for all faculty recruiting processes.

ETH Zurich is one of the leading international universities for technology and the natural sciences. It is well known for its excellent education, ground-breaking fundamental research and for implementing its results directly into practice.Founded in 1855, ETH Zurich today has more than 18,500 students from over 110 countries, including 4,000 doctoral students. To researchers, it offers an inspiring working environment, to students, a comprehensive education.Twenty-one Nobel Laureates have studied, taught or conducted research at ETH Zurich, underlining the excellent reputation of the university.

The Faculty Recruiting Tool is now distributed and developed by frentix under the name Selectus as a standard software. frentix GmbH is a small company located in Zurich which is specialized in development and operating of e-learning solutions, Java and JavaScript software development and multimedia and e-learning productions based on the Open Source Learning Management System OpenOLAT. frentix is a spin-off company of University of Zurich. frentix offers its services to various institutions in public education sector as well as to private corporations all over the world. In 2015 frentix was awarded as 'vendor of the year' in the category 'LMS' for its outstanding client oriented services".
